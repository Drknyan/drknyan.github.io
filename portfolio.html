Character Creation Rules


1.] Characters can be any species or race imaginable within reason. Characters must be balanced with the system. I.E: the character race, if other than human, must have some detriment to offset the benefits in the beginning, such as demons being weak to light and holy magics in general.
2.] You may have starting equipment, however it must be within reason and can not be something like a god slaying sword or something.
3.] If you think of a skill you believe would be an intrinsic skill to a race you want your character to be, ask staff to help balance it in the ⁠character-advice channel.
4.] User’s may create their own magic spells but they must be approved by Staff before use.
5.] If you wish to start with a Unique Skill, Consult with staff.
6.] Staff NPC's Are not necessarily bound by normal Character Creation Rules, these are approved by @Drako Vishious in a different character submission channel.
